About TrajectoryVis

TrajectoryVis is produced as part of an end-of-studies project at the National School of Computer Science on the theme «The visualization of spatio-temporal trajectories on Twitter »

TrajectoryVis, allows you to visualize the trajectories of the hashtags # Covid19 and #Corona during the period May, June 2020 with several visualization techniques.
