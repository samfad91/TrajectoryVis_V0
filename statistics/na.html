This 3D histogram of the continent of NORTH AMERICA is represented on three axes (x, y, z),

On the X axis we have represented the countries of the NORTH AMERICA continent; that appear in the tabs.

We can see quite simply, that the quantity of tweets located in

United States is the highest in the two periods May and June 2020.
