Histogram 3D ASIA

Explanation:

This 3D histogram of the continent of ASIA is represented on three axes (x, y, z),

such as: X: the place; Y: the number of tweets; Z: time.

On the X axis we represent the countries of the continent of ASIA; that appear in the tabs.

We can see quite simply that the number of tweets located in

Japan (JP) and Malaysia (MY) is the highest in the period of May 2020, and India (IN) in June 2020
