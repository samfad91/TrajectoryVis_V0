Explanation:

This 3D histogram of the continents is represented on three axes (x, y, z),

such as: X: the location; y: the number of tweets; z: time.

in this first 3D Histogram we have to arrange in relation to the continent, that is to say we have represented, the quantity of tweets (localized)

in the 6 existing continents, in the two time periods May, and June 2020.

We can see quite simply, that the quantity of tweets located in the continent

North America is highest in the period of May 2020, and Europe and North America in June 2020.
